About Pages for Change

Pages for Change (P4C) is a non-profit organization dedicated to breaking the cycle of poverty through literacy. We believe that access to books is a fundamental right, not a privilege.

Our journey began with a simple observation: millions of books gather dust on shelves while millions of children have nothing to read. We bridge this gap by collecting, refurbishing, and distributing books to underfunded schools and community centers.

Our Mission

To empower communities by providing accessible literature and fostering a lifelong love for reading through dedicated outreach and charitable support.

We believe that every book has the potential to spark a revolution in a young mind. Our mission is to ensure those sparks fly everywhere.

VISION ✦

Our Vision

A world where every individual has the opportunity to learn, grow, and succeed through the power of books, regardless of their circumstances.

✧

Imagine a future where libraries are as common as streetlights, and literacy is a fundamental right for all. That is the world we are building.

VALUES ❈

Core Values

Integrity is at our core, ensuring honesty in every page we provide. We champion Accessibility because we believe in books for everyone. Our commitment to Education drives our goal of lifelong learning.
